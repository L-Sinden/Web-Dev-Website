Here are some of the best arcades in Ireland

Barcadia video arcade down in cork offers a large selection of arcade games and great food and beverages on tap it also has an awesome retro vibe. It is open 5 till late most days and 1 till late on saturdays

Barcadia Location

Vr Link Dublin is a Virtual reality arcade down in dublin they offer a large selection of vr games for both solo players and large groups it is open from 11 till 6:30 most days and 11 till 9 on thursdays

VR Link Dublin Location

The Leisuredome is a arcade and amusements venue down in Meath it offers a large selection of arcade games and all are cashless they use a card that you top up. They offer a good selection of food and beverages. It is open from 11:30 till 8 most days and from 10:30 till 9 on saturdays
